History

Hero

Headline: "Our History"
Subhead: "Over a Century of Growth and Innovation"
Intro: "From a single wooden elevator in 1906 to a diversified agricultural enterprise, Arthur Companies has grown alongside the communities we serve for over a century."

Historical Milestones

1906: "The First Elevator - Arthur Companies is founded when the first wooden grain elevator is built alongside the Northern Pacific rail line in Arthur, ND. Local farmers finally had a place to sell their grain without traveling long distances."
1927: "Surviving the Great Depression - Despite economic hardship across the country, Arthur Companies maintains operations through diversification and community support, becoming a pillar of stability in the region."
1948: "Second-Generation Stewardship - The second generation expands storage capacity and begins offering agronomy inputs to neighboring growers, marking the company's first steps into agricultural services beyond grain handling."
1965: "Modernization Era - Arthur Companies invests in its first concrete grain storage facilities, significantly increasing capacity and improving grain quality preservation for customers."
1983: "Tech Roots – Great Plains Software - Members of the Arthur family help launch Great Plains Software, sowing the seeds for a culture of innovation that still guides the company and establishing technology as a core value."
1995: "Expansion to Hunter - Arthur Companies acquires its second location in Hunter, ND, beginning a period of strategic growth that would eventually span multiple states and service areas."
2001: "Microsoft Acquisition - Great Plains Software is acquired by Microsoft and becomes Microsoft Business Solutions, providing capital that fuels further growth at Arthur and reinforcing the value of strategic partnerships."
2008: "Precision Agriculture Pioneer - Arthur becomes one of the first ag retailers in the region to offer precision agriculture services, including variable rate application and GPS-guided equipment."
2015: "Venture Investment Platform - The company establishes its venture investment arm, focusing on agricultural technology startups that align with the company's vision for the future of farming."
2020: "Research & Development Focus - Arthur expands its research capabilities with dedicated trial plots and partnerships with universities to validate new technologies and practices."
Today: "Where Innovation Grows - Arthur operates modern grain, agronomy, venture and research platforms across the northern plains, focused on helping growers and partners thrive from the ground up with a continued commitment to community and innovation."
